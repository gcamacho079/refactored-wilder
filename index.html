About

The Wilder (Portland, OR) plays fast, loud, high-energy rock 'n roll with a sound that sparks a mood of inspiration, and is dusted with influences by vintage groups such as Deep Purple, CCR, Foghat and The Beatles.

Fans have described us as The Black Keys on speed.
